01 · Problem

No platform. No roadmap. No precedent.

Zenni had no B2B2C platform, no enterprise portal, and no way for organizations to offer eyewear benefits to their employees. Healthcare companies, universities, and eye care providers needed a scalable solution — but the infrastructure to serve them didn't exist.

02 · My Role

What I owned.

Led the full 0→1 build across three product lines — Vision Benefits, Safety Eyewear, and Corporate Gifting. Owned product strategy, roadmap, and cross-functional execution across Engineering, Design, QA, Analytics, and AI/ML from concept to live platform.

03 · Approach

How I built it.

  • Engaged healthcare, university, and eye care provider stakeholders directly to validate assumptions before building
  • Led an AI-powered computer vision product using facial scan data to deliver personalized eyewear recommendations
  • Built the enterprise analytics layer that gave clients real-time utilization data — became the deciding factor in contract sign-offs, driving 25% client conversion lift
  • Designed the self-serve onboarding portal that cut client time-to-value from weeks to minutes and reduced admin effort by 30%
  • Compressed launch timelines by 2 months through disciplined dependency management and cross-functional alignment
  • Represented the platform at Shoptalk Las Vegas and Vision Expo New York — demoed live, pitched to 500+ enterprise buyers
