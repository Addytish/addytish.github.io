01 · Problem

Full ownership. No handoff. No runway.

The experimentation program was already running across multiple brands when I stepped in — but there was no knowledge transfer, no documentation handoff, and no ramp-up period. I had to self-navigate a live, high-velocity program across 5 brands simultaneously while building stakeholder trust from day one.

02 · My Role

What I owned vs. contributed to.

Owned: Full A/B experimentation program — strategy, test calendar, cross-functional execution, and weekly executive readouts across all 5 brands.

Contributed to: Salesforce Einstein AI personalization — running live campaigns, configuring personalization rules, and defining recommendation surfaces across web and mobile.

03 · Approach

How I ran it.

  • Defined experimentation roadmap across 5 brands — opportunity sizing, hypothesis development, and test prioritization
  • Coordinated cross-functional execution across Engineering, Analytics, and Brand teams to keep 40–45 tests per brand per year on track
  • Presented go/no-go/iterate/stop recommendations to brand and executive leadership — grounded in statistical rigor and business context
  • Managed multi-brand dependencies where test pipelines, engineering capacity, and brand calendars intersect simultaneously
  • Ran live Salesforce Einstein personalization campaigns — recently-viewed carousels, best seller modules, and content targeting across web and mobile
