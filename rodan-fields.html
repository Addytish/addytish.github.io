01 · Problem

No Mini Cart. High abandonment. Revenue left on the table.

Rodan + Fields had no embedded checkout experience. Cart abandonment was high, cross-sell opportunities were missed, and mobile checkout friction was directly impacting revenue across a 500K+ user base.

02 · My Role

What I owned.

Led the Mini Cart from concept to launch — full ownership of product strategy, UX definition, user research, and cross-functional execution. Also owned personalized promotions, subscription tier structures, and auto-ship billing logic.

03 · Approach

How I built it.

  • Conducted remote user research sessions to map checkout friction points and validate assumptions before building
  • Ran funnel analysis to identify highest drop-off stages and prioritize fixes
  • Led Mini Cart build from zero — embedded checkout integrating full payment flow, promotional savings, perks display, and subscription options in one experience
  • Launched personalized promotions for 500K+ users based on purchase history and subscription tier
  • Owned subscription tier structures and auto-ship billing logic to support retention mechanics
  • A/B tested every checkout change — validated against cart abandonment and cross-sell metrics before shipping
